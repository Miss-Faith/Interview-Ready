30 Seconds

The idea of a 30-second interview doesn't mean that you literally are interviewed for only 30 seconds. Instead, it covers situations where you meet potential employers, such as at career fairs or even while riding an elevator, and you only have 30 seconds to sell yourself and encourage them to set up a formal interview with you. Selling yourself in just 30 seconds is tough, but it can be done.
