30 Seconds

The idea of a 30-second interview doesn't mean that you are or will be interviewed for only 30 seconds.
Rather it refers to cases where you need to "sell" yourself in 30 seconds say to encourage an interviewer or client to set up a future meeting.
What would you say to capture their attention?
Sounds tough and is probably tougher in practice, but it can be done.
Tread carefully... This article is for asignment purposes


What to Do

Be Yourself.
You are either qualified or not.
Plus it would be nice working in a place that values your authentic self

What to Avoid

Not being prepared.
We all love winning.
The worst feeling you can go home with is knowing you didn't put your best foot forward whether you get the job or not
